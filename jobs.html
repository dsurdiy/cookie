HAUTE COUTURE COOKIES

Jobs

At Cookie Couture, our secret ingredient is you! Our Cookie Crew is the heart and soul of every sweet moment. With their passion, smiles, and dedication, they truly add the perfect finishing touch to every dessert. Join today and help us in our mission of bringing friends and family together over a box of the best cookies in the world!

Whether you want to climb the leadership ladder at your location or eventually join the corporate team, we want to support your growth! As a baker or driver, you’ll strive to provide high-quality desserts by processing orders, balling dough, topping desserts, and occasionally driving.

Cookie Couture is the fastest-growing cookie and dessert company in the nation and we offer a fun and inclusive work environment, where you’ll get to help us become the best brand in the nation. Plus you’ll get FREE cookies!

Applicants must be at least 16 years old, have a knack for customer service, and love cookies. Full-time and part-time positions are available and pay varies at each location. Crew members must be able to stand, walk, bend, and occasionally lift heavy items.

About you

You love customer service and do everything to make everyone feel comfortable with us: exceptionally good service is very important to us.

  • You are trustworthy, reliable and punctual
  • You speak German & English
  • Experience with a portafilter machine or any barista skills are an advantage
  • Experience as a waitress in restaurants or cafés is advantageous
  • You love donuts & good coffee
  • You are available at least 3 days per week (we don't offer mini-jobs - you would need to available at least 12-20 hours per week)

Ready to turn your love of cookies into a dream job? Fill out our application and join the Cookie Couture Crew today!

What we offer you
  • Hourly wage 12,20 Euro to start, 12,57 Euro after successful training period
  • Different and flexible time modules (working student, part-time, full-time, varying from approx. 12-20, 20-30 or 30-37.5 hours per week)
  • Coffee and barista training with our customer service and coffee trainer
  • Tax-free Sunday and public holiday allowances (25-50%)
  • Attractive subsidies for the HVV monthly ticket (public transport)
  • Good planning: you will receive your shift schedule one month in advance
