HAUTE COUTURE COOKIES

Catering

Our catering crew is gearing up once again to bring the best of Cookies Events & Catering right to your doorstep!

Whether it’s a corporate gathering, an intimate anniversary, or any celebration in between, our Bar Crew and catering team is ready to serve up delicious cocktails and treats tailored to your event’s size and style, ensuring a memorable experience for you and your guests.

This season, we’re excited to highlight ODE and Laori, both Berlin-based and featured in our spritz versions for a light and refreshing taste, perfect for those seeking alcohol-free options. Additionally, our cocktail lineup is graced by the Cardamom Cooler, a harmonious blend of cardamom and mint, not only for their health properties but also as perfect, refreshing ingredients for the summer.

Among our culinary highlights, Chef Ronja skillfully prepares dishes that celebrate the freshest, most vibrant ingredients available. Among these seasonal delights, our Pea Tartare is a true standout. This dish, with its bright combination of Grapefruit, Sesame Seeds, and Pea Sprouts, epitomizes our dedication to bringing the season’s bounty to your plate in the most delicious way possible.

FOOD CATERING

Fresh ingredients are the priority in all dishes – homemade with careful preparation they get your taste buds dancing. Known and beloved creations get a dash of the extraordinary. Our focus is on special vegan and vegetarian dishes and on exceptional fish and meat creations from sustainable production.

  • Regional, seasonal and special ingredients
  • Cooperation with selected local farmers
  • Seated dinner, flying food, concept food, BBQ, street food, picnic
  • Individually tailored food concepts
  • Capacity: from candle light dinner for two up to a grandiose gala with 2000 guests
